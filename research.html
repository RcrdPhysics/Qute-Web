Research Areas

Quantum Computing

expand_more

Our group focuses on developing robust and scalable quantum computing hardware and algorithms. We explore various physical implementations, from superconducting circuits to trapped ions, aiming to build fault-tolerant quantum machines.

Key Projects:

  • Developing Noise-Resilient Qubits
  • Quantum Error Correction Codes
  • Hybrid Quantum-Classical Algorithms

Quantum Communication

expand_more

We are pioneering new methods for secure communication based on the principles of quantum mechanics. Our research includes quantum key distribution (QKD), quantum repeaters, and the development of a future quantum internet.

Key Projects:

  • Long-Distance Quantum Cryptography
  • Entanglement Swapping for Quantum Networks
  • Device-Independent QKD Protocols

Quantum Sensing

expand_more

This area of our research leverages the extreme sensitivity of quantum systems to create sensors with unprecedented precision. Applications range from medical imaging and navigation to fundamental physics experiments.

Key Projects:

  • Nitrogen-Vacancy (NV) Center Magnetometry
  • Quantum-Enhanced Imaging
  • Atomic Clocks for GPS-free Navigation
